About

This is the extended gallery page for Rescued.Art Thrift Store Art Gallery. Welcome! I am Jake and most everything pictured here I have acquired from thrift stores around the San Antonio area. I drive around a lot and try to go into any nearby thrift stores whenever I can. That doing has led to many found treasures and everything you see pictured below.
This page is as much an advertisement for the pieces and gallery as it is a record of my collection and the pieces and friends that came through my doors.

The gallery is open every First Friday from about 7pm-11 at Blue Star Arts Complex, behind Contemporary, at 117 Blue Star. Come for art, wine, and friends--feel to hang out!

Otherwise, I can open the gallery by apt. only--email jake@rescued.art for inquiries. Pieces are for sale or rent, and we offer short term event curation services.
