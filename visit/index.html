Visit & Events

I’m Jake. Most everything in the archive I found in thrift stores around San Antonio — I drive a lot, and I stop in wherever I can. That habit has turned up a lot of treasures, and the gallery is as much a record of the collection as it is a place to find your next piece.

Come hang out — there’s art, wine, and friends, and no pressure to do anything but enjoy it.

First Fridays

Open every First Friday, ~7–11pm, at Blue Star Arts Complex — 117 Blue Star, behind Contemporary.

By appointment

Want to come another time? Email jake@rescued.art and we’ll find a slot.

Events & curation
